Autis Tracker UI

Welcome to Autis Tracker - your all-in-one tool for tracking and observing the daily activities of children and students with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). Our user-friendly interface and minimalist design make it suitable for users of all ages.

Autis Tracker empowers parents, caregivers, and educators to monitor and analyze the behaviors and routines of individuals with ASD. With its bright and vibrant color theme, it creates a positive and engaging experience for both users and those being observed.

  • Effortlessly record and track various activities and behaviors throughout the day.
  • Customize categories and tags to suit individual needs and preferences.
  • Visualize data through easy-to-understand charts and graphs for better insights and understanding.
  • Collaborate with other caregivers and educators by sharing observations and progress reports.

Whether you're a parent keeping track of your child's progress, a teacher observing students in the classroom, or a therapist monitoring therapy sessions, Autis Tracker is your reliable companion. Start simplifying your tracking and observation tasks today - download Autis Tracker and make a positive difference in the lives of individuals with ASD.
